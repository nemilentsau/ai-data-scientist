A regularized logistic regression slightly outperformed a random forest in repeated stratified CV, with mean ROC AUC `0.8618` vs `0.8505`, and achieved holdout ROC AUC `0.8519`. Diagnostics did flag a modest set of influential, confidently misclassified cases worth reviewing for hidden subgroups or label noise.
