Main result: the data is structurally clean, but `funding_amount_usd` is heavily right-skewed with influential high-end rounds, so a log-target model is the defensible choice. The strongest signals are `years_since_founding`, `employees`, and `revenue_growth_pct`; sector contributes little after adjustment; only `Series_C` is clearly above `Seed` once the other variables are controlled for. The selected ridge model achieved about `R² = 0.385` on the holdout set, with mild remaining heteroskedasticity and some influential observations noted explicitly in the report.
